The Kibo Experience

Technology is transforming every area of our lives, and education should be no different. Learning at Kibo prepares you for leadership and impact in a globally competitive world.

Better Together

At Kibo, we're driven by the belief that unity sparks innovation and progress. Our core value, "Better Together," fuels collaboration, celebrates diversity, and fosters a culture where collective efforts shine. Join us in shaping a brighter future through the power of togetherness.

Learn Continuously

At Kibo, we're driven by the belief that constant learning fuels innovation and growth. Our "Learn Continuously" core value inspires us to embrace curiosity, adapt to change, and turn challenges into stepping stones for success.

Rise To The Challenge

At Kibo, we believe in pushing beyond comfort to achieve greatness. "Rise to the Challenge" - because your potential knows no limits.

Experience at Kibo

As a novice in computer science,with zero knowledge in coding or programming,I often thought of how I wouldn't lag behind in studies.Well..with measures Kibo has in place for its students I can confidently say this is no more my worry.With each passing day I am very optimistic of the new knowledge I will acquire since there is a community on which I rely.Through Kibo,I can now realize my dream of playing a role in an advanced tomorrow.

As a young girl, I have always been interested in technology, I wanted to know how it works, then I came across KIBO School of Technology where I took the Try Kibo course for free and I must say that KIBO is more than just a School. It is a whole community that you can interact with everyone, build your knowledge from scratch, develop yourself. KIBO School of Technology is a community that will build you and continue to make you learn continously.

I loved the growing technology since I was 9 years old and I had a dream to read and explore more on the growing technology. My family background made it difficult for me to make my dreams true but Kibo School of Technology came to my rescue. It made it simpler for me persue the course of my dream. As we are speaking I am now steps ahead and I believe that I am meant for greatness and all thanks to Kibo School. It is an institution that really natures dreams and make them possible.

Stephanie Edzie Computer Science Student
Jhon Smith Computer Science Student
Joram Computer Science Student
